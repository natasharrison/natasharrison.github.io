about her.


Natasha Harrison holds a MBA from the University of Arizona, where she also earned her bachelor’s degrees in Marketing and Fine Arts. Since 2014, she has worked in the Athletics Department at that same university. During her time there, Natasha has overseen the department’s community outreach initiatives and has coordinated multiple special events.

She hopes to continue to use her determination and thirst for learning to turn her career into being a Software Developer.

Natasha lives in Tucson, AZ with her fiancé, Joey Murata, and their two (fat) cats, Cocoa and Louis. She runs on coffee, lives to travel, and enjoys quality time with family, friends and nature.
